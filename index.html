Basic Timer & Stopwatch Apps

Here are instructions for using these Apps in a full screen web browser while duplicating your display.

These can be used on or offline.

Step 1: Duplicating your display

  1. Connect your computer to a second display or projector.
  2. Right-click on your desktop and select "Display settings."
  3. Under "Multiple displays," select "Duplicate these displays."
  4. Your computer screen should now be duplicated on the second display.
  1. Connect your computer to a second display or projector.
  2. Click on the Apple menu and select "System Preferences."
  3. Click on "Displays" and then select the "Arrangement" tab.
  4. Check the box next to "Mirror Displays."
  5. Your computer screen should now be duplicated on the second display.

Step 2: Making your browser full screen

  1. Press F11 on your keyboard to enter full screen mode.
  2. To exit full screen mode, press F11 again.

  2. Select "Fullscreen" from the drop-down menu.
  3. To exit full screen mode, press the Esc key on your keyboard.

  2. Select "Enter Full Screen."
  3. To exit full screen mode, move your cursor to the top of the screen and click on the green button in the top left corner of the window.

Using these Apps

Launch Timer
Once open, enter the time to countdown from at the bottom of the window.
The warning time sets the time which the screen will turn red.
Press "Start" to begin, and "Stop" to end.
Once time ends, you'll need to "Reset" to start a new timer.
Pressing "Warn" will instantly turn the screen red. You can not go back to normal without resetting.

Launch Stopwatch
Once open, clicking "Start" will start the stopwatch
Top stop, press the "Stop" button
The "Reset" button resets the stopwatch to zero
"Toggle MS" will toggle the display of milliseconds on screen
